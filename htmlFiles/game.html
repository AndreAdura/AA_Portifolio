Learn to Count(HTML)

This is a simple HTML game designed to engage children that are learning how to count; The game was developed with the Mobile First concept and its ready to be played in a Touch Screen Device. The Game Logic is make the player get rid off all numbers by clicking each one from the smaller to the bigger number.



Play
Clicking at the Play button you will be directed to a new Tab where the Game will be started.

Art Alley - AA(Unity)

This is a project created by me to integrate a variety of art works from Interior Designers, Graphic Designers, 3D Modelers and Generalists in the same place. The idea is to integrate all the jobs in a 3D environment creating an alley with the Houses, Art Galleries and also the 3D Characters walking around the area.
